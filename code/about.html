About Us

We want to help pet parents to have better life by implementing innovative and healthy life style for their pet.

Mission / Origin

Whole box is founded by two pets owner and friends, Ada and Erick in 1948. Their business idea inspired by their adorable and picky pets Amber (golden retriever, 7yrs) and Pishe( grumpy cat, 4yrs). They are dedicated to providing pet parents easy and healthy solution for their loving pets.
